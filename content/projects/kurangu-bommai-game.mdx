## Overview

Created as the official mobile game for the Tamil film *Kurangu Bommai*, this maze-based puzzle experience features five uniquely colored characters, each tied to a corresponding gate mechanism. The game was built under extreme time constraints to amplify buzz during the movie's theatrical promotion window.

## The Challenge

Film promotional games often suffer from generic gameplay that fails to create meaningful engagement with the film's brand. The challenge was to create a game with distinctive mechanics and strong thematic integration while working within an extremely compressed five-day development timeline.

## Technical Approach

Designed and implemented the complete game solo, from concept through deployment. Created custom maze generation algorithms and color-matching puzzle mechanics that aligned with the film's thematic elements.

## Key Implementations

- **Solo Development:** Independently handled concept, mechanics, design, and deployment — delivering the entire project within 5 days to meet film promotion timelines without sacrificing quality.

- **Game Logic & Mechanics:** Implemented a color-matching puzzle system where characters can only pass through gates of their own color, encouraging strategic planning and multi-step route optimization.

- **Level & Maze Design:** Built multiple handcrafted maze layouts with progressive difficulty and character coordination challenges to maintain engagement across the game's duration.

- **Touch Controls & Optimization:** Designed mobile-friendly touch input with swipe gestures and ensured smooth performance across a wide range of Android devices, from budget phones to flagship devices.

- **Thematic Integration:** Synchronized game visuals and audio with the branding and narrative tone of Kurangu Bommai, delivering a consistent cross-media experience for film fans.

## Outcome

The game achieved significant download volume during the film's theatrical window, generating social media buzz and reinforcing brand awareness across the film's target demographic. The strong thematic integration was praised by both players and film promotional teams.
