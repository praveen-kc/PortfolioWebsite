## Overview

Built as a comprehensive virtual driving simulator for NISSAN Driving Institute, Kidzania, Kuwait. The project enabled users — especially young learners — to practice essential driving skills in a safe and controlled 3D environment, supporting training in car control, traffic rules, and situational awareness.

## The Challenge

Traditional driving instruction faces constraints around availability, weather conditions, and safety during early learning stages. Young learners benefit from extensive practice in varied scenarios before handling real vehicles. The challenge was to create an engaging simulator that taught practical skills while maintaining the appeal of an entertainment experience.

## Technical Approach

Developed gameplay systems for vehicle physics, traffic behavior, and learner evaluation. Built intuitive instructional interfaces that guided new drivers through progressive skill development.

## Key Implementations

- **Gameplay Systems Development:** Contributed to vehicle mechanics including acceleration, braking, steering, and realistic physics-based movement that responded authentically to user input.

- **Traffic Logic & Scenario Integration:** Assisted in designing and integrating driving environments such as highways, intersections, and city streets with AI-driven dynamic traffic behavior that responded realistically to player actions.

- **UI/UX Elements for Guidance:** Built instructional UI overlays and HUD components to provide real-time prompts, navigation aids, and rule-based error alerts to support learning objectives.

- **Feedback & Evaluation Logic:** Supported development of trainee evaluation systems tracking performance metrics like speed compliance, signal usage, and reaction times for comprehensive progress reporting.

- **Testing & Optimization:** Participated in playtesting and optimization to ensure consistent simulation quality across varied PC hardware setups deployed in entertainment center environments.

## Outcome

The simulator became a popular attraction at Kidzania locations, introducing young visitors to driving fundamentals while reinforcing NISSAN's brand positioning. Trainees who completed simulator sessions demonstrated improved performance in real driving lessons.
