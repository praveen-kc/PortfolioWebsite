## Overview

Developed for a corporate event by MSD (Merck & Co., Inc.) in Dubai, this interactive racing game used Kinect-based gesture controls for an immersive body-driven gameplay experience. Player car behavior was dynamically influenced by individual and team sales performance data, creating a personalized, gamified reflection of real-world metrics.

## The Challenge

Corporate events struggle to create meaningful engagement that connects company values with memorable experiences. Traditional gamification often feels superficial. The challenge was to create an experience where employees felt genuinely connected to both the game and their professional achievements, with seamless integration of business metrics.

## Technical Approach

Implemented real-time integration between Microsoft Kinect SDK for motion capture and game mechanics that responded to sales data APIs. Designed dynamic track and obstacle generation based on performance profiles.

## Key Implementations

- **Kinect Motion Integration:** Implemented body gesture controls for steering and speed using Microsoft Kinect SDK, allowing intuitive and responsive gameplay through full-body movement without traditional controllers.

- **Sales Data-Driven Gameplay:** Integrated employee KPIs into game mechanics — modifying car speed, fuel pickups, and obstacle density based on team or individual sales metrics to create personalized race experiences.

- **Dynamic Track Generation:** Designed logic to spawn track elements conditionally, ensuring unique race conditions for each player based on performance data profiles and real-time game state.

- **Data Pipeline & Leaderboards:** Developed secure systems to ingest and translate performance data into in-game stats, live leaderboards, and pre-race UI highlights showing team rankings.

- **Event Deployment Optimization:** Tuned the build for seamless plug-and-play experience with minimal calibration, ensuring smooth operation across multiple participant shifts during the multi-day event.

- **Brand Integration:** Incorporated MSD's brand visuals and campaign identity across UI, HUD, and track environments to ensure consistency with event marketing goals.

## Outcome

The game was a highlight of the corporate event, generating significant employee engagement and positive feedback. The data-driven personalization created memorable moments where professional achievements directly influenced gameplay, reinforcing connections between individual effort and team success.
