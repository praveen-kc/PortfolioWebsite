## Overview

Lykagluv redefines the retail shopping journey by allowing users to virtually try on clothing and accessories using customizable 3D avatars. With facial capture and measurement-based avatar generation, the app delivers a realistic, immersive try-before-you-buy experience, enhancing convenience in both online and in-store environments.

## The Challenge

Online apparel retail suffers from high return rates driven by fit uncertainty and inability to try products before purchase. Customers lack confidence in sizing decisions without physical try-on experiences. The challenge was to create a convincing virtual try-on system that users would trust for purchase decisions.

## Technical Approach

Led the development of avatar customization systems with measurement-based generation and facial photo mapping. Coordinated with e-commerce teams to integrate try-on experiences with product catalogs and checkout flows.

## Key Implementations

- **Project Leadership & Team Coordination:** Managed a compact team of one Unity developer and two 3D artists, overseeing timelines, task tracking, and delivery schedules for the mobile app launch.

- **Avatar Customization & Measurement Mapping:** Guided the implementation of scalable avatars generated from user-entered body measurements, ensuring accurate garment fitting and realistic simulation across diverse body types.

- **Facial Capture Integration:** Led integration of face photo mapping onto avatars to personalize the virtual try-on experience and increase relatability for users seeing themselves in the garments.

- **E-Commerce & Try-On Flow Integration:** Coordinated with the e-commerce team to link try-on experiences to real-time product inventory, enabling seamless in-app purchases with size recommendations based on avatar fit.

- **Performance Optimization:** Optimized rendering pipelines for mobile GPUs to maintain smooth frame rates during garment simulation and avatar animation.

## Outcome

Partner retailers reported significant reductions in return rates and increases in average order value. The try-on experience increased customer confidence in online apparel purchases, particularly for first-time customers unfamiliar with brand sizing.
