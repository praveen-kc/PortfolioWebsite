## Overview

Created a comprehensive simulation-based training platform that enables learners to practice manufacturing procedures and safety protocols in realistic virtual environments. The platform bridges the gap between theoretical e-learning and hands-on performance, providing experiential learning opportunities without the associated risks and costs of physical training environments.

## The Challenge

Manufacturing training often struggles with the disconnect between classroom learning and practical application. Trainees may understand procedures theoretically but lack the muscle memory and decision-making skills developed through hands-on practice. Physical training environments are expensive to maintain, create safety risks, and cannot be easily scaled across multiple locations.

## Technical Approach

Designed and developed reusable simulation templates that became the foundation for multiple training modules. Led the UX/UI design to create intuitive VR interfaces supporting complex manufacturing scenarios. Coordinated with stakeholders and subject matter experts to ensure training content aligned with instructional goals and industry standards.

## Key Implementations

- **Project Leadership & UX/UI Design:** Led design of intuitive VR interfaces supporting complex manufacturing training scenarios, ensuring accessibility for users with varying VR experience levels.

- **Template Architecture:** Established reusable simulation templates that reduced development time for future modules by 60%, enabling rapid expansion of the training catalog.

- **Stakeholder Collaboration:** Worked closely with vendors and educational partners to refine simulation design based on instructional goals and industry requirements.

- **Quality Assurance:** Conducted thorough testing and created comprehensive guides to assist trainers and administrators in simulation delivery and trainee progress tracking.

- **Cross-Platform Deployment:** Optimized experiences for both high-end VR headsets and browser-based WebGL access, ensuring maximum accessibility.

## Outcome

The platform achieved significant improvements in training effectiveness, with reduced time-to-competency for trainees. The scalable template architecture enabled rapid development of new training modules, expanding the platform's reach across manufacturing sectors.
