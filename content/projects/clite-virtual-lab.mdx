## Overview

Built a flexible, modular platform that replicates real-world laboratory setups virtually, enabling learners to explore science experiments using interactive apparatus on PC and Android. Designed to promote safety, accessibility, and engagement through hands-on learning simulations that closely mimic actual lab conditions.

## The Challenge

Physical laboratories present safety risks, require expensive equipment, and cannot scale to meet demand during peak learning periods. Students in remote or underserved areas often lack access to quality lab experiences. The challenge was to create virtual labs that maintained the tactile learning benefits of physical experiments while being universally accessible.

## Technical Approach

Developed a modular experiment architecture where each lab simulation operates as a self-contained module. Worked with subject matter experts to ensure educational accuracy while maintaining engaging interactive experiences.

## Key Implementations

- **Interactive Experiment Simulation:** Developed core experiments involving physics interactions, variable control, and apparatus behavior using real-time simulations with accurate physics modeling.

- **UI Redesign with NGUI:** Migrated the UI from Unity's built-in system to NGUI for performance gains and improved responsiveness across device types, reducing frame drops during complex interactions.

- **Cross-Platform Deployment:** Optimized input systems, resolution scaling, and frame performance for seamless experience on both PC and Android platforms with diverse hardware specifications.

- **Modular Architecture:** Designed experiments as self-contained modules enabling scalable integration and curriculum-specific customization, allowing institutions to select experiments relevant to their courses.

- **Academic Collaboration:** Worked alongside subject matter experts to ensure each virtual lab met educational standards and pedagogical accuracy, with learning outcomes aligned to curriculum requirements.

## Outcome

The virtual lab platform achieved widespread adoption across educational institutions, particularly during periods of remote learning. The modular approach allowed schools to build customized lab curricula while significantly reducing equipment and consumable costs.
