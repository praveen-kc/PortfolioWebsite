## Overview

Developed an interactive 3D WebGL application designed to assess learners' understanding of key training concepts in a simulated environment. By combining immersive visuals with contextual scenarios, the platform increased user engagement and comprehension while enabling instructors to evaluate knowledge retention more effectively than traditional multiple-choice assessments.

## The Challenge

Post-training assessments often suffer from low engagement and limited ability to measure practical understanding. Traditional quizzes fail to capture how well learners can apply knowledge in realistic scenarios. The challenge was to create an assessment tool that felt engaging and relevant while providing meaningful data about learner competency.

## Technical Approach

Led the design and integration of 3D environments that presented contextual questions and visual cues within a browser-friendly WebGL framework. Coordinated with the design team to create scenarios that mimicked real-world situations where training concepts would be applied.

## Key Implementations

- **Project Planning & Execution:** Defined project goals, milestones, and timelines in collaboration with stakeholders. Maintained comprehensive development plans to ensure timely delivery within budget constraints.

- **Interactive 3D Experience:** Led the design and integration of 3D environments that presented contextual questions and visual cues within a browser-friendly WebGL framework, ensuring broad accessibility without requiring VR hardware.

- **Team Coordination:** Assigned tasks, monitored progress, and managed workload across Unity developers, designers, and testers to align implementation with educational outcomes.

- **QA & Cross-Browser Compatibility:** Defined testing criteria and worked closely with QA to ensure reliable performance across multiple devices, browsers, and operating systems.

- **User Feedback Integration:** Incorporated trainer and learner feedback through multiple iteration cycles to improve clarity, responsiveness, and engagement metrics.

## Outcome

The platform demonstrated significant improvements in knowledge retention measurement, with assessment scores correlating more strongly with practical performance than traditional methods. The interactive approach increased completion rates and provided richer data for instructional improvement.
