## Overview

Developed a browser-based 3D application that allows users to design and customize their home interiors with fine-grained control — from furniture arrangement and decor to wall finishes and accents. Built on Unity WebGL, the tool enables real-time visualization and integrates with an e-commerce platform, allowing users to purchase products directly within the design experience.

## The Challenge

Home furnishing retailers struggled to bridge the gap between online browsing and in-store purchase decisions. Customers often couldn't visualize how products would look in their spaces, leading to uncertainty and high return rates. The challenge was to create an accessible, performant 3D design tool that worked in web browsers while connecting seamlessly to product catalogs and checkout systems.

## Technical Approach

Led a cross-functional team of three Unity developers and two 3D artists. Architected the core design tools and coordinated with web engineers to connect Unity-based design tools with Magento's e-commerce backend. Implemented modular content loading to support expanding product catalogs from multiple vendors.

## Key Implementations

- **Team Leadership:** Led a cross-functional team of three Unity developers and two 3D artists. Conducted stand-ups, resolved blockers, and maintained team velocity across design and implementation phases.

- **Custom Free-Draw & Layout Tool:** Designed an interactive drawing system enabling users to freely create, reposition, and adjust layout designs post-creation, with intuitive snap-to-grid and measurement guides.

- **WebGL & Magento Integration:** Worked closely with web engineers to connect Unity-based design tools with Magento's e-commerce backend, enabling real-time product selection, pricing, and cart updates without page reloads.

- **Modular Content Loading:** Architected scalable loading of dynamic furniture and decor assets to support expanding product catalogs from multiple vendors, with automatic LOD adjustment based on connection speed.

- **Performance Optimization:** Ensured smooth experience across browsers using mesh instancing, occlusion culling, and efficient bundling tailored to WebGL runtime limitations, achieving sub-3-second initial load times.

## Outcome

The integrated design and shopping experience significantly improved conversion rates. By allowing customers to visualize products in context before purchase, return rates decreased substantially. The platform became a key differentiator for the client's e-commerce strategy.
